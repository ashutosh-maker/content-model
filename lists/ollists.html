INSTALLING COURSERA APP FROM PLAYSTORE ON PHONE

  1. press the side button
  2. enter your password
  3. phone has unlocked
    1. click on menu
    2. click on playstore
    3. click on search icon on upper left corner
    4. type "coursera"
    5. click install
    6. wait till download completes
    7. click install
    8. you are done!
